Our Story

More than a team —
a brotherhood

Men In Blue CC was founded in 2022 by a group of cricket-passionate individuals who wanted to bring the spirit of the game to Waterloo, Ontario. What started as weekend pickup games has grown into a competitive side in the SOCA league.

We represent the diversity and passion of our community. From seasoned club players to new talent, every member of MIB brings something unique to the field — and the dressing room.
